Prepare for your visit

Is this for your wedding? For best results a practice session is required at least 10 days prior to the final service.

    Before Airbrush:

  • Beauty services such as waxing, manicures, facials, and massages should take place before your spray tan.
  • Shave legs one day prior to your tan

    Preparation:

  • Exfoliate your skin before your appointment
  • Use an aggressive exfoliator like exfoliating gloves
  • Make sure your skin is clean and dry
  • Avoid using lotion or oil on your skin

    Clothing:

  • Wear loose, dark colored clothing after your tan
  • The more covered up you are, the safer your tan will be
  • Choose loose pants and a loose t-shirt over short shorts and a tank top.
